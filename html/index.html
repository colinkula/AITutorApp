Computer Science AI Tutor

About the System

Our platform harnesses the advanced capabilities of ChatGPT's AI to tutor Computer Science students of all levels. The AI Chat Box is thoughtfully designed to tailor explanations based on each user's knowledge level, ensuring clarity and understanding. By leveraging the user's Computer Science background, our AI delivers explanations in a way that's accessible and easy to comprehend.

This tool is perfect for breaking down complex concepts and empowering students to tackle challenges with confidence. Unlike traditional solutions, our AI doesn't just provide answers - it acts as a tutor, guiding students to independently solve coding problems and deepen their understanding.

Ready to elevate your learning? Visit the AI Chat Box page to experience it firsthand! Or, keep reading for detailed instructions on navigating our website.

How to Use the AI Chat Box

  1. 1. Go to the AI Chat Box: Click on the AI Chat Box link in the navigation menu or use the button below to access the page.
  2. 2. Choose Your Knowledge Level: Select your level of experience with Computer Science. Choose from Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced.
  3. 3. Ask a Question: Type your question in the prompt box and click the Submit button. You can ask the AI to explain any Computer Science topic you're curious about.
    Note: The AI will explain concepts but will not give exact coding solutions.
  4. 4. Read the Response: After submitting your question, the AI will show its response in the box below.
    Note: The response may be cut off due to a limit on the number of tokens. The token count represents the "cost" of using the ChatGPT API.
    5. Try It Now!

How to Use the AI History

  1. 1. Go to the AI History: Click on the AI History link in the navigation menu or use the button below to visit the page.
  2. 2. Choose a Sorting Filter: Select how you want to sort your history: Date, Time, Cost, or Prompt.
  3. 3. Apply the Filter: After selecting your filter, click the Submit button to apply the filter and view the history.
  4. 4. View the History: All your previous questions and AI responses will be listed according to the filter you selected.
    5. Try It Now!
